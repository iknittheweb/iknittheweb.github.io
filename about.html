I am an aspiring web developer on a mission to create mind-blowing digital experiences; I'm obsessed with HTML, CSS, and a sprinkle of JavaScript magic. 🪄

I believe that creativity and problem-solving go hand in hand, and I bring this philosophy to my work as a web developer.

I am developing my skills in HTML and CSS, as well as dipping my toes into Javascript. I have studied Bootstrap 5, SASS, and other frameworks, and I use what I have learned to create my own personal templates.

I have earned level 1 certifications in HTML, CSS, and Javascript from the Cambridge Certification Authority, completion of sololearn courses in HTML, CSS, and Web Development Fundamentals, and Kevin Powell's Conquering Responsive Layouts course.

I also earned the developer certification in Responsive Web Design at freecodecamp.org and have completed the Frontend Masters Bootcamp.

When I'm not coding, you’ll find me🧶knitting cozy creations or getting lost in a good book.🕮

Feel free to check out my portfolio and contact me if you have any questions or would like to collaborate on a project! Let’s get together and turn ideas into reality!
